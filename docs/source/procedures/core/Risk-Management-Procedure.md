# 1. Purpose

This procedure defines the process for identifying, analyzing, evaluating, treating, monitoring, and reporting organizational risks.

The objective is to ensure that risks affecting governance, security, architecture, operations, and compliance are systematically managed and traceable.

---

# 2. Scope

This procedure applies to:

- Governance Risks
- Security Risks
- Architecture Risks
- Operational Risks
- Compliance Risks
- Technology Risks
- Repository Risks
- Project Risks

# 4. Roles and Responsibilities

## Risk Owner

Responsible for:

- Risk identification
- Risk analysis
- Risk treatment
- Risk monitoring

## Governance Authority

Responsible for:

- Risk acceptance
- Risk escalation
- Governance oversight

## Control Owner

Responsible for:

- Implementing mitigation controls
- Providing risk reduction evidence

---

# 5. Risk Lifecycle

Each risk SHALL follow:

Identification
        ↓
Classification
        ↓
Analysis
        ↓
Evaluation
        ↓
Treatment Planning
        ↓
Mitigation
        ↓
Monitoring
        ↓
Review
        ↓
Closure

---

# 6. Risk Identification

Each risk record SHALL contain:

- Risk ID
- Risk Name
- Description
- Owner
- Category
- Source
- Date Identified
- Status

---

# 7. Risk Classification

Risks MAY be classified as:

- Strategic Risks
- Governance Risks
- Security Risks
- Technical Risks
- Operational Risks
- Compliance Risks
- Financial Risks

---

# 8. Risk Analysis

Risk analysis SHALL evaluate:

- Likelihood
- Impact
- Severity
- Existing Controls
- Residual Risk

---

# 9. Risk Evaluation

Risk evaluation SHALL determine:

- Risk Priority
- Acceptance Criteria
- Required Treatment
- Escalation Requirements

---

# 10. Risk Treatment

Risk treatment options MAY include:

- Avoid
- Mitigate
- Transfer
- Accept

Treatment plans SHALL define:

- Action Owner
- Required Controls
- Target Date
- Verification Method

---

# 11. Risk Traceability

Risks SHALL maintain relationships with:

---

# 12. Risk Monitoring

Risk monitoring SHALL evaluate:

- Risk Status
- Control Effectiveness
- Residual Risk
- New Risk Conditions
- Treatment Progress

---

# 13. Risk Reporting

Risk reports SHALL contain:

- Risk Summary
- Assessment Results
- Treatment Actions
- Ownership
- Current Status
- Approval Records

---

# 14. Risk Change Management

Risk changes SHALL include:

- Change Description
- Impact Assessment
- Updated Risk Level
- Approval Record
- Version Update

---

# 15. Risk Exceptions

Risk exceptions SHALL require:

- Documented Justification
- Risk Acceptance
- Governance Approval
- Review Schedule

---

# 16. Metrics

Risk management SHOULD measure:

- Open Risks
- Risk Reduction Rate
- Treatment Completion
- Residual Risk Level
- Risk Review Compliance
