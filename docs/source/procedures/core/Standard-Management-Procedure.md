## Version

v0.1

## Status

# 1. Purpose

This procedure defines the process for creating, reviewing, approving, publishing, maintaining, and retiring organizational standards.

The objective is to ensure that standards provide consistent, measurable, and enforceable guidance across governance, architecture, security, and operational domains.

---

# 2. Scope

This procedure applies to:

- Governance Standards
- Security Standards
- Architecture Standards
- Technology Standards
- Documentation Standards
- Repository Standards
- Operational Standards
- Compliance Standards

---

# 3. Authority

This procedure derives authority from:

Enterprise Constitution
        ↓
Governance Framework
        ↓

        ↓
Standard Management Procedure

---

# 4. Roles and Responsibilities

## Standard Owner

Responsible for:

- Standard creation
- Standard maintenance
- Periodic review
- Technical accuracy

## Governance Authority

Responsible for:

- Standard approval
- Governance alignment
- Compliance oversight

## Domain Experts

Responsible for:

- Technical validation
- Implementation guidance
- Review participation

---

        ↓

---

# 6. Standard Identification

Each standard SHALL contain:

- Standard ID
- Standard Name
- Purpose
- Scope
- Owner
- Classification
- Version
- Effective Date
- Review Date
- Status

---

# 7. Standard Development

Standards SHALL consider:

- Governance Requirements
- Policies
- Architecture Principles
- Security Requirements
- Industry Practices
- Compliance Obligations

---

# 8. Standard Review Process

Standards SHALL be reviewed for:

- Technical Accuracy
- Applicability
- Consistency
- Security Impact
- Compliance Alignment

---

# 9. Standard Approval

Approval SHALL verify:

- Authority Alignment
- Technical Feasibility
- Operational Impact
- Control Applicability

Approved standards SHALL receive official publication status.

---

# 10. Standard Implementation

Implementation SHALL define:

- Applicable Systems
- Required Controls
- Responsible Owners
- Verification Methods
- Required Evidence

---

# 11. Standard Traceability

Standards SHALL maintain relationships with:

        ↓

---

# 12. Standard Change Management

Standard changes SHALL include:

- Change Description
- Technical Impact
- Risk Assessment
- Review Approval
- Version Update

---

# 13. Standard Exceptions

Exceptions SHALL require:

- Exception Record
- Business Justification
- Risk Assessment
- Approval Authority
- Expiration Date

---

# 14. Metrics

Standard management SHOULD measure:

- Standard Coverage
- Adoption Rate
- Verification Completion
- Exception Frequency
- Review Compliance
