# LUXIONEX Evidence Collection Procedure

## Version

v0.1

## Status

Draft — Foundational Evidence Collection Procedure

---

# 1. Purpose

This procedure defines the process for identifying, collecting, validating, storing, maintaining, and retaining governance evidence.

The objective is to ensure that every governance activity, control implementation, and verification result is supported by reliable and traceable evidence.

---

# 2. Scope

This procedure applies to:

- Governance Evidence
- Security Evidence
- Architecture Evidence
- Control Evidence
- Verification Evidence
- Compliance Evidence
- Audit Evidence
- Repository Evidence

# 4. Roles and Responsibilities

## Evidence Owner

Responsible for:

- Evidence generation
- Evidence accuracy
- Evidence maintenance
- Evidence availability

## Verification Owner

Responsible for:

- Evidence validation
- Evidence completeness review
- Verification reporting

## Governance Authority

Responsible for:

- Evidence retention requirements
- Audit readiness
- Governance oversight

# 5. Evidence Lifecycle

Each evidence record SHALL follow:

Identification
        ↓
Collection
        ↓
Classification
        ↓
Validation
        ↓
Storage
        ↓
Review
        ↓
Retention
        ↓
Archiving
        ↓
Disposal

---

# 6. Evidence Identification

Each evidence record SHALL contain:

- Evidence ID
- Evidence Name
- Description
- Owner
- Source
- Classification
- Creation Date
- Version
- Status

---

# 7. Evidence Classification

Evidence MAY be classified as:

- Requirement Evidence
- Policy Evidence
- Standard Evidence
- Procedure Evidence
- Control Evidence
- Verification Evidence
- Compliance Evidence
- Audit Evidence

---

# 8. Evidence Collection Process

Evidence collection SHALL define:

- Evidence Source
- Collection Method
- Collection Frequency
- Responsible Owner
- Validation Criteria
- Storage Location

---

# 9. Evidence Validation

Evidence SHALL be validated for:

- Authenticity
- Completeness
- Accuracy
- Integrity
- Traceability
- Availability

---

# 10. Evidence Storage

Evidence storage SHALL ensure:

- Access Control
- Integrity Protection
- Version Management
- Backup Availability
- Retention Compliance

---

# 11. Evidence Traceability

Evidence SHALL maintain relationships with:

Requirement
        ↓
Policy
        ↓
Standard
        ↓
Specification
        ↓
Procedure
        ↓
Control
        ↓
Verification
        ↓
Evidence

---

# 12. Evidence Retention

Evidence retention SHALL define:

- Retention Period
- Storage Requirements
- Review Schedule
- Disposal Process

---

# 13. Evidence Change Management

Evidence changes SHALL include:

- Change Description
- Change Reason
- Updated Version
- Approval Record

---

# 14. Evidence Exceptions

Evidence exceptions SHALL require:

- Documented Justification
- Risk Assessment
- Governance Approval
- Review Schedule

---

# 15. Metrics

Evidence management SHOULD measure:

- Evidence Coverage
- Evidence Completeness
- Validation Success Rate
- Retention Compliance
- Audit Readiness
