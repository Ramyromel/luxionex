# LUXIONEX Evidence Specification

## Version

v0.1

## Status

Draft — Foundational Evidence Specification

---

# 1. Purpose

This specification defines the required structure, metadata, quality requirements, and lifecycle for governance evidence within LUXIONEX.

The objective is to ensure that evidence is reliable, traceable, verifiable, auditable, and sufficient to demonstrate governance compliance.

This specification derives authority from:

Enterprise Constitution
        ↓
Governance Framework
        ↓
Evidence Governance Standard
        ↓
Evidence Specification

---

# 2. Scope

This specification applies to:

- Governance Evidence
- Security Evidence
- Architecture Evidence
- Repository Evidence
- Compliance Evidence
- Verification Evidence
- Audit Evidence

---

# 3. Evidence Objectives

Every evidence artifact SHALL:

- Demonstrate objective facts
- Support governance decisions
- Enable independent verification
- Preserve traceability
- Maintain integrity
- Remain auditable

Evidence SHALL avoid ambiguity.

---

# 4. Required Metadata

Every evidence artifact SHALL define:

- Evidence Identifier
- Evidence Title
- Version
- Status
- Evidence Owner
- Collection Date
- Classification
- Related Requirement
- Related Control

---

# 5. Required Structure

Evidence SHOULD include:

- Purpose
- Source
- Collection Method
- Description
- Verification Status
- Integrity Information
- References

---

# 6. Evidence Requirements

Every evidence artifact SHALL define:

- What was collected
- Where it originated
- Who collected it
- When it was collected
- How it was verified
- Why it is relevant

Evidence SHALL be reproducible whenever possible.

---

# 7. Governance Alignment

Every evidence artifact SHALL align with:

- Enterprise Constitution
- Governance Framework
- Applicable Standards
- Applicable Policies
- Related Controls
- Supporting Procedures

Evidence SHALL support governance objectives.

---

# 8. Traceability

Evidence SHALL maintain traceability to:

Requirement
        ↓
Policy
        ↓
Control
        ↓
Procedure
        ↓
Verification
        ↓
Evidence

Every relationship SHALL remain auditable.

---

# 9. Verification

Evidence MAY be verified through:

- Technical Review
- Manual Inspection
- Independent Validation
- Security Assessment
- Compliance Review
- Internal Audit

Verification results SHALL be documented.

---

# 10. Compliance

Compliance SHALL evaluate:

- Evidence completeness
- Evidence integrity
- Evidence authenticity
- Traceability
- Verification status

Insufficient evidence SHALL require remediation.

---

# 11. Evidence Lifecycle

Evidence SHALL follow the lifecycle:

1. Collection
2. Validation
3. Review
4. Approval
5. Storage
6. Retention
7. Archival
8. Disposal

Historical evidence SHALL remain preserved according to retention requirements.

---

# 12. Exceptions

Exceptions require:

- Documented justification
- Risk assessment
- Governance approval
- Defined review schedule

Approved exceptions SHALL remain traceable.
